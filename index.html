Deadline Hub tracks deadlines for conferences, journals, training programs, and more.

About This Website

This website is managed and updated solely by Matt DeVerna. It aims to be a useful resource for researchers and students in computational social science (broadly defined).

Matt's long-term goal is to automate the process of finding deadlines for conferences, journals, and other relevant events. In the future, the backend will use artificial intelligence to scrape and update these deadlines directly from official websites.
